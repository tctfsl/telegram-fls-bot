How to shut down or restart your Menara PC remotely
1. Go to Start (Windows icon on the taskbar).
2. Type "cmd".
3. Click "cmd" in the search result list. This will launch the command prompt.
4. Type:
	- "shutdown /s" to shut down, OR
	- "shutdown /r" to restart.
5. Press Enter.
6. You will get a message saying that the PC will shutdown in a minutes.
7. If you're restarting, please wait 10-15 minutes before you try to remote again.
